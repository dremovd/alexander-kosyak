Biography

Born 08.12.1957, Russia.

1970 – 1974 School of Arts, Pyatigorsk city.

1979 – 1984 Kosygin State University of Russia (Technologies. Design. Arts).

Since 1985 has been taking part in exhibitions, since 1987 began to participate in foreign exhibitions.

Since 1991 member of Moscow Artists Union, member of Russian Artists Union.
